## What I Teach

### Outdoor Yoga
Yoga practiced where it belongs — in parks, gardens, and open spaces. Gentle flows that connect you to the ground beneath you. All levels welcome.

### Kids Yoga
Certified kids yoga instructor. Sessions blend movement, storytelling, and mindfulness in a way that feels like play. Perfect for preschools, community centers, and family events.

### Gentle & Restorative Yoga
Slow, supported practice focused on deep rest and nervous system regulation. Ideal for beginners or anyone who needs to slow down.

### Yoga Nidra
Guided deep relaxation — you lie down and I guide you into conscious rest. Often described as "the most relaxed you've ever been while awake."
