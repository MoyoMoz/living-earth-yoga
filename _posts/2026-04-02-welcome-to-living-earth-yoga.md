This is the beginning.

I've been practicing yoga for over sixteen years. I've taught in studios, trained with masters, and studied ancient texts. But the moments that changed me most happened outside — barefoot in the grass at sunrise, breathing with the trees, feeling my body as part of the earth rather than separate from it.

Living Earth Yoga is my attempt to share that feeling.

## What this space is

This is a place for people who want more from their yoga practice than a workout. It's for anyone who's ever looked up during savasana and wished the ceiling was the sky.

Here you'll find:
- Cinematic yoga videos that feel like a moving painting
- Blog posts about practice, nature, and slow living
- Classes and workshops — including kids yoga
- An invitation to step outside and practice with the earth

## What this space is not

This isn't about perfect poses or expensive leggings. There's no pressure to perform. Living Earth Yoga is for beginners and experienced practitioners alike — for anyone who wants to slow down and reconnect.

## What's next

I'm shooting new content every day. The first kids yoga workshop is coming April 15th. The blog will grow. The videos will come. And through it all, the earth will be here — patient, generous, alive.

Thank you for being here at the start.

With love and light,
Moyo
