I'm Moyo, a yoga teacher, mother, and lifelong student of the earth.

I received my first 200-hour yoga teacher certification in 2010, and I'm currently deepening my practice through a 500-hour training at DRIFT. I'm also recently certified to teach kids yoga — because I believe the seeds of mindfulness are best planted young.

## Why "Living Earth"?

Yoga changed my life, but it wasn't in a studio that it clicked the deepest. It was outside — feet in the grass, wind on my skin, sun warming my face — that I truly understood what it means to be present.

> The earth is not a backdrop to your practice. It is your practice.

Living Earth Yoga is my invitation to you: come outside. Unroll your mat in the garden. Practice beneath the trees. Let the birds be your soundtrack and the sky be your ceiling.

## What you'll find here

- **Blog posts** about yoga, nature, and living slowly
- **Classes and workshops** — including kids yoga
- **Video content** that feels like a moving painting — cinematic, quiet, and nature-forward
- **A community** of people who believe the best yoga happens outside

## My path

I didn't take the straight road here. I started in kitchens — cooking was my first love. Then I found code through Ada Developers Academy and worked as a software engineer. Through it all, yoga was the constant. Now I'm weaving all of it together.

I'm a mom first. Everything I build has to make room for that. Living Earth Yoga is designed to grow gently — like a garden, not a factory.
